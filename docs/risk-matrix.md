# Risk Matrix

| Risk | Likelihood | Severity | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Incorrect model output | Medium | Medium | Monitor outputs and add review process |
| Input anomaly | Medium | Medium | Add input validation and anomaly tracking |
| Data drift | Medium | High | Monitor drift and retrain when needed |
| Privacy exposure | Low | High | Use synthetic data and avoid sensitive inputs |
| System downtime | Low | Medium | Track errors, latency, and request failures |
| Lack of auditability | Low | Medium | Maintain structured audit logs |
