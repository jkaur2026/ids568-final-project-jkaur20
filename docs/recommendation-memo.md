## Summary of Results

The A/B test simulation compared the baseline model with a new model variant. The baseline model achieved an accuracy of approximately 0.774, while the new model achieved an accuracy of approximately 0.852.

The observed uplift in performance is 0.078, indicating that the new model performs significantly better.

## Statistical Significance

The p-value from the t-test is 0.0015, which is well below the threshold of 0.05. This confirms that the improvement in performance is statistically significant and not due to random chance.

## Recommendation

Based on the results, it is recommended to deploy the new model. The model demonstrates a clear improvement in accuracy and meets the criteria for statistical significance.
